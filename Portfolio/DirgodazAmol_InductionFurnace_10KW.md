## 10KW Induction Furnace
Half-Bridge 1-Phase Induction Furnace.  
Power Controller with a dimmer.  

Client from [Dirgodaz Amol Industries Inc., Iran](https://dirgodazamol.com/en/)  
Work type was on-site  
Jan 2017 - Jul 2017  
  
Note: It was a prototype.    

My Tasks:
- The design and make of these boards
  - Single Phase Dimmer Based on Analog
  - Single Phase Dimmer Based on AVR MCU
  - 12V/5A Full Bridge DC Motor Driver Based with MOSFETs and Isoleted Inpout
  - Controller Board base on AVR MCU
  - Display Panel with 20×4 Character LCD
- Electrical components installing
